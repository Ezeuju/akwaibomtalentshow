    About Us

    Who we are

    We’re all about discovering amazing talents that come from the Akwa Ibom Talent Foundation Inc., based in the USA, and we’re proudly registered as an NGO in Nigeria. Our goal? To shine a bright light on talents and give our country a big boost through the magic of talent discovery; what we termed “Akwa Ibom Talent Show” otherwise known as AKTS. Imagine this: We’re like a launching pad for folks with awesome talents. We give them a stage to show off what they’ve got to the whole world. AKTS is like a big burst of inspiration that helps everyday people become superstars, even if they don’t have the cash to put themselves out there.

    Our Vision

    Our vision for the Akwa Ibom Talent Show is to empower every young individual to unlock their unique potential, leading them to purposeful and joyful lives. By nurturing their talents, we aim to pave the path towards a society free from street crime, where mental well-being flourishes for all.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to harness our own talents as a catalyst for empowering individuals of all ages to explore and cultivate their unique gifts. For those who are yet to uncover their talents, we offer a transformative training program guided by AKTS volunteer coaches. This program equips individuals to evolve into skilled professionals with a distinct purpose, arming them with an impressive CV that continues to grow. We extend opportunities for talent development, enabling them to embrace a life of fulfillment and accomplishment.
