About Us

Who we are

We’re all about discovering amazing talents that come from the Akwa Ibom Talent Foundation Inc., based in the USA, and we’re proudly registered as an NGO in Nigeria. Our goal? To shine a bright light on talents and give our country a big boost through the magic of talent discovery; what we termed “Akwa Ibom Talent Show” otherwise known as AKTS. Imagine this: We’re like a launching pad for folks with awesome talents. We give them a stage to show off what they’ve got to the whole world. AKTS is like a big burst of inspiration that helps everyday people become superstars, even if they don’t have the cash to put themselves out there. We take the best talents and give them the royal treatment, helping them become super successful through our special program. But that’s not all – we’re also here for young go-getters struggling to make their dreams come true in their careers and businesses. Here’s the cool part: AKTS isn’t just any talent show. We’re the first of our kind, spreading our wings from the United States to Nigeria and beyond. We’re like a global arena where rising stars get to shine. They get to show off their unique voices to the whole world, and people get to vote for their favorites! In a world where lots of amazing talents don’t get their chance, we’re here to change that. Akwa Ibom Talent Show believes that if you’ve got talent, you should get a chance to be heard. That’s why we’re here, making sure talented folks can get the support they need with just a few clicks.

Our Vision

Our vision for the Akwa Ibom Talent Show is to empower every young individual to unlock their unique potential, leading them to purposeful and joyful lives. By nurturing their talents, we aim to pave the path towards a society free from street crime, where mental well-being flourishes for all.

Our Mission

Our mission is to harness our own talents as a catalyst for empowering individuals of all ages to explore and cultivate their unique gifts. For those who are yet to uncover their talents, we offer a transformative training program guided by AKTS volunteer coaches. This program equips individuals to evolve into skilled professionals with a distinct purpose, arming them with an impressive CV that continues to grow. We extend opportunities for talent development, enabling them to embrace a life of fulfillment and accomplishment.
